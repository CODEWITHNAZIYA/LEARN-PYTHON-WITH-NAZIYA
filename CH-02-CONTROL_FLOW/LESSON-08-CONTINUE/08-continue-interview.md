# Quick Answers

**Q:** What is continue?

**A:** Continue skips the current iteration and moves to the next iteration.

---

**Q:** Does continue stop the loop?

**A:** No.

---

**Q:** Which loops support continue?

**A:** Both for and while loops.

---

**Q:** Can continue be used outside a loop?

**A:** No.

---

**Q:** What is the main difference between break and continue?

**A:** Break exits the loop, while continue skips the current iteration and continues the loop.
